Student Test List View

When a student navigates to his or her tests. He or she will see the following dialog.

Figure 1: Student View of Graded Tests

When the student opens a test for taking, they are presented with the screen in Figure 1. There will be a rows and columns view of each test showing: grade, date taken, a name of test. Clicking "View Details" will show each question with the score for that question and teacher comment (if given).
