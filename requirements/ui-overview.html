2.1 UI Overview

The Test Tool application is broken up into three main sections: Tests (Section 2.2 ,Section 2.3, and Section 2.4), Questions (Section 2.5), and Grading and Taking tests (Section 2.6 and Section 2.7)

Screens under the Tests catagory involve creating, viewing, and editing Tests. This is the biggest part of the app.

Screens under the Questions catagory involve creating, viewing, and editing questions. They also include the overall Question Bank available for use to the instructor in all tests.

Screens under the Grading and Taking tests involve the view students will see when they take tests, and how the instructor will interact with these tests once the students have taken them.
