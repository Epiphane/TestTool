2.5.3. Question Bank Example

The instructor navigates to the question bank, and is presented with the table of questions, as shown in Figure 1.

Figure 1: Question Bank - Table.

The instructor scrolls the table until the question: "What is your favorite color comes onto the screen". The instructor then clicks the question for editing, which opens the screen in Figure 2.

Figure 2: Edit Question.

The instructor decides to move this question from the class "CPE 307" to the class "CPE 308", so he/she clicks the drop down menu in the top left which says "CPE 307", leading to the visual presented in Figure 3, and clicks "CPE 308" from the dropdown.

Figure 3: Edit Question Subject.

Finally, the question clicks the "Save" button, returning him/her to the question table, and selects the checkbox next to the recently modified question. The instructor then clicks "Make Test", intending to create a new, one-question test. The instructor is presented with the screen shown in Figure 4, which prompts for test information.

Figure 4: Creating a New Test.

The instructor clicks "Create Test", and the test is successfully added to their list of tests, available for publishing or editing.
