2.5.1. List of Questions

When the instructor navigates to the question bank, he/she is presented with the screen in Figure 1.

Figure 1: Empty Question Bank.

Since the teacher has not made questions or tests yet, the question bank is empty. The list of questions saved in the question bank is presented in a table view providing a brief overview of all the questions available. The instructor has the option of clicking a question to open it in the Question Editing screen, or selecting one or more questions and clicking the "Make Test" button to create a new test and open the New Test Options screen.
